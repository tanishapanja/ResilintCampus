# ResilintCampus
Stay one step ahead of disaster. Resilient Campus delivers real-time alerts for earthquakes, floods, cyclones, heatwaves, and forest fires. Send your live GPS location as an SOS to campus admins in seconds. Explore live incident links, stay aware, and keep your entire campus community safe.
🌍 Disaster Emergency Support App

A smart and fast emergency response application designed to help people during disasters and critical situations. This app provides live location emergency alerts, instant safety precautions, real-time disaster news, and interactive quizzes to spread awareness and improve preparedness.


---

🚨 Key Features

📍 Live Location Emergency Alert

With just one tap, users can instantly send their live location to emergency contacts or administrators during disasters or dangerous situations for faster rescue and support.

⚠️ One-Step Safety Solutions

Get immediate access to:

Disaster precautions

Emergency guidelines

Survival tips

Safety instructions


The app provides quick and simple actions users can follow during emergencies like floods, earthquakes, cyclones, fires, and more.

📰 Real-Time Disaster News

Stay updated with the latest:

Disaster alerts

Emergency updates

Safety announcements

Government notifications


Users can quickly access important information during ongoing situations.

🧠 Interactive Awareness Quiz

An engaging quiz section helps users:

Learn disaster management

Test emergency knowledge

Improve awareness

Prepare for real-life situations


📱 User-Friendly Interface

Simple, clean, and responsive design that allows users to navigate quickly during stressful situations.
